Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01/31/2025

At Havilah Medical and Wellness, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal health information is handled with care and respect. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information in compliance with applicable laws, including the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA).

Information We Collect
  • Personal Information: We collect personal information that you provide to us, including your name, address, phone number, email address, date of birth, and insurance information
  • Medical Information: We collect health information relevant to your care, including medical history, diagnoses, treatments, and medications.
How We Use Your Information
  • To provide, manage, and coordinate your healthcare.
  • To communicate with you regarding appointments, test results, and treatment plans.
  • To bill and process payments for our services.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Healthcare providers involved in your care.
  • Insurance companies for billing purposes.
  • Regulatory agencies as required by law.
  • Third-party service providers who assist us in delivering healthcare services.
Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and request copies of your medical records.
  • Request amendments to your health information.
  • Receive an accounting of disclosures of your health information.
  • Request restrictions on certain uses and disclosures of your information.
  • Request communication of your health information by alternative means.
Protecting Your Information

We implement appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal health information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on our website, and we will notify you of significant changes.
